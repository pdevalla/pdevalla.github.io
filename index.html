Interests

Aswath Damodaran's Blog - Musings on Markets

Professor Damodaran is a professor of finance at the Stern School of Business at New York University. His blog as well as his website www.damodaran.com provides overviews of industries as well as in-depth analysis and valuations for companies in the news. This allows real world application of the valuation techniques and financial analysis that I learned in the classroom.

Dealbook

Dealbook is a site that publishes news and analysis on financial markets and events. The coverage is both timely and thorough and is what I use to keep up to date with current events in the financial world. Andrew Ross Sorkin is the founder and overseer of the site.
